About JOOUST

Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST) is a premier institution of higher learning in Kenya, dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and community service.

Established in 2013, the university has grown to become a center of academic excellence with state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant student community. Our programs are designed to meet the needs of the 21st century job market.
