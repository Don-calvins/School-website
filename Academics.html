About the University

JOOUST is a center of excellence in training, research, and community outreach. Named after the first Vice President of Kenya, the university focuses on science and technology as the drivers for sustainable development.

Our Academic Schools

School of Informatics

Pioneering innovation in Information Security, Computer Science, and Data Analytics.

School of Agricultural Sciences

Focusing on food security through the INSEFOODS World Bank Center of Excellence.

School of Health Sciences

Advancing Public Health and Epidemiology in the Lake Victoria Basin.

School of Engineering

Developing sustainable infrastructure and renewable energy solutions.
