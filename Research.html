Our Research Vision

JOOUST is committed to conducting cutting-edge research that addresses critical challenges in science and technology. Our research initiatives span multiple disciplines and contribute significantly to regional and global knowledge advancement.

We partner with leading institutions worldwide to foster collaborative research and innovation.

Key Research Centers

INSEFOODS - Insect Science, Engineering and Food Security

Developing sustainable food sources through insect farming and processing technology. Our research focuses on nutritional security and environmental sustainability in East Africa.

Blue Economy Directorate

Promoting sustainable use of marine and water resources for economic growth. Our research contributes to fisheries management, aquaculture development, and ocean conservation.

Institute of Technology Innovation

Fostering innovation in biotechnology, engineering, and information technology. This center bridges academic research with industrial applications.

Environmental Science Research Unit

Conducting research on climate change, renewable energy, and sustainable development. Our initiatives support the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
