Application Process

JOOUST welcomes applications from qualified students around the world. Our admissions process is designed to identify students with strong academic potential and a passion for science and technology.

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit required documents and academic transcripts
  3. Pay the non-refundable application fee
  4. Await admission decision (within 4-6 weeks)
  5. Accept admission offer and register

Undergraduate Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Valid identification document
  • English proficiency test (if applicable)
  • Completed application form

Postgraduate Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Letters of recommendation (3)
  • Statement of purpose
  • GMAT/GRE scores (where applicable)
