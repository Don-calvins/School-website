Student Support Services

Welcome to Your Student Journey

At JOOUST, we're committed to supporting your academic and personal development. Our comprehensive student services ensure you have the resources you need to succeed.

Key Student Services

Academic Support

Tutoring services, writing centers, and academic coaching to help you excel in your studies.

Health & Wellness

Campus health services, counseling, and recreational facilities for your well-being.

Career Services

Job placement, internship opportunities, and professional development workshops.

Technology Support

IT help desk, computer labs, and technology training for all students.

Student Life

Clubs, societies, and campus activities for a vibrant student community.

Housing Services

On-campus accommodation options and residential life support.

Important Student Portals

  • E-Learning Portal - Access course materials and submit assignments
  • Student Email - Official university communication channel
  • Library Resources - Access thousands of academic journals and books
  • Student Portal - Register for courses and check grades
  • Campus WiFi - Available in all academic buildings

Financial Information

Tuition Payment: Payments can be made through the student portal or in person at the Finance Office.

Financial Aid: Various grant and loan programs are available for eligible students.

Scholarships: Merit and need-based scholarships are offered each academic year.

Payment Plans: Flexible payment plans available for tuition.

Important Dates

Semester Dates:

  • Semester 1: September - December
  • Semester 2: January - April

Registration: Open 2 weeks before semester begins

Add/Drop Period: 2 weeks from semester start

Student Rights & Responsibilities

As a JOOUST student, you have rights and responsibilities within our academic community:

Your Rights:

  • Access to quality education and academic resources
  • Fair treatment and non-discrimination
  • Privacy and confidentiality of your records
  • Due process in disciplinary proceedings

Your Responsibilities:

  • Maintain academic integrity and honesty
  • Follow campus policies and regulations
  • Respect the rights of others
  • Contribute positively to the campus community
